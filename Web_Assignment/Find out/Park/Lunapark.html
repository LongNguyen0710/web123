Luna Park

Time Out says

Welcome to Luna Park Melbourne, an iconic amusement park nestled in St Kilda, Melbourne's vibrant coastal suburb. Since its inception in 1912, Luna Park has been a beloved destination, recognized by its famous giant smiling face entrance.

Discover a thrilling array of rides and attractions, including the historic Scenic Railway, the world's oldest continuously operating wooden roller coaster. From adrenaline-pumping rides like the Pharaoh's Curse to family-friendly classics such as the Carousel, Luna Park offers entertainment for all ages. Don't miss the chance to experience the magic of Luna Park during your visit to Melbourne
