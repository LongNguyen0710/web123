Melbourne Zoo

Time Out says

Welcome to Melbourne Zoo, Australia's oldest zoo, nestled in the heart of Royal Park. Home to over 320 species, the zoo offers a captivating journey through diverse habitats, from the lush rainforests to the African savannah.

Explore immersive exhibits and get up close with wildlife, including lions, elephants, and koalas. Engage in conservation efforts and educational programs while enjoying the serene beauty of the zoo's gardens. A visit to Melbourne Zoo promises unforgettable encounters with nature for visitors of all ages.

Written by Nicola Dowse
Tuesday 12 October 2023
