Crown Melbourne

Time Out says

Welcome to Crown Melbourne, a premier entertainment complex located on the south bank of the Yarra River. Experience luxury at its finest with world-class hotels, fine dining restaurants, and a sophisticated casino offering a range of gaming options.

Indulge in live entertainment, including captivating shows and vibrant nightlife venues. Crown Melbourne also boasts a variety of shopping outlets and spa facilities for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Whether you're seeking excitement or relaxation, Crown Melbourne offers an unforgettable experience for every visitor.
